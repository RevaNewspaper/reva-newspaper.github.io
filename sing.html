  • Internship program at National University Singapore

Ten students from our REVA university attended academic internship program held in National University Singapore . This program was conducted by Corporate Gurukul, Singapore and delivered with professionals from Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) Education and faculty from National University of Singapore (NUS). The curriculum included Big Data and Hadoop System Administration by Hewlett-Packard Enterprises and Big Data Analytics using Artificial Neural Networks delivered by professors of National University of Singapore. On the HPE front the learning process was more of a hands-on approach which enabled our students to understand and implement the concepts better. The final project consisted of setting up single and multi - node clusters using Cloudera Manager, running different services of Hadoop and analysing a dataset and deriving results from it using Hive Queries. The concepts of Artificial Neural Networks was introduced using R Programming and Python, several algorithms for Data Analytics and various applications of ANN like Sentiment analysis were implemented. The Final Project was based on application of Machine Learning Algorithms to perform Sentiment Analysis and other Data Analytics on a given dataset. All the students were awarded 3 certificates and personalized Letter of Evaluation by the professors of NUS after successful completion of the Internship. Our students had an interaction with students from institutes like IIT, BITS, SRM etc from all over India and with the professors of NUS.
